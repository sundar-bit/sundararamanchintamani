📚 RagaChintamani Author

In 2005, Sundararaman released RagaChintamani — a unique music manual that maps classical ragas used in Tamil film songs. Over 1,800 songs are classified under 160 ragas, with detailed arohanam, avarohanam, and special characteristics for each raga — creating an invaluable bridge between classical and popular music.

The book has sold 1,500+ copies and has been recommended by Annamalai University as a reference book for its BA Music course.

🚗 Sholinganallur IT Campus — Rear Gate Issue

In the Sholinganallur ELCOT IT campus, more than 3 major IT companies (HCL, Wipro, Mahindra Satyam, and now Cognizant) employ around 1,000 people who commute daily by car. During peak hours, employees spend more than 10-20 minutes at the Sholinganallur signal both morning and evening.

The ELCOT campus has a rear gate connecting to OMR that is deliberately kept closed, citing toll revenue concerns. Sundararaman calculated that opening this gate would save:

  • Fuel Cost: ~Rs. 99 Lakhs/year (idling at signal)
  • Lost Productivity: 500 man-months/year (~Rs. 5 Crores)
  • Total Annual Loss: ~Rs. 6 Crores

🎓 Social Project Initiator — OK Google Project

The "OK Google" Project by Sundararaman is an initiative to use Voice Assisted Smart Speakers for school children. In March 2019, Sundararaman experimented by installing a "Google Home Mini" gadget at one of the Corporation schools in Chennai.

The results were remarkable — the average General Knowledge score of students before implementation was 35.8%. After installation and guidance, the average score increased to 65.8% in just 3 weeks — a 30% improvement without using computers or laptops!

🎯 Benefits of the Initiative
  • General Knowledge of students enhanced by 30% using just a small gadget like Google Home Mini or Amazon Alexa
  • Students learn the art of asking the right questions on multiple topics
  • Students learn to use state-of-the-art technologies, preparing them for the future

To implement this idea across the state, Sundararaman handed over a proposal to the Honorable Education Minister of Tamil Nadu, Mr. K.A. Sengottaiyan on June 13, 2019.
